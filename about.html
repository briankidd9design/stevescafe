Steve's Cafe

Steve and Marlene Hofer

Here at Steve's Cafe, we have been cooking up home-style breakfast and lunch in the heart of downtown Bothell for more than fifteen years. We are also a great place to stop by and grab a delicious cup of locally roasted Tully's Coffee.

Located on historic Main Street Bothell, Steve's Cafe, is owned and operated by us: a husband and wife team that not only knows the importance of a great meal at a reasonable price, but also the importance of family and community.

So whether you are just passing through or have been in the area for some time-whether you are looking for a quick cup of coffee or a full meal-we graciously invite you to visit us and look forward to serving you.

--Steve and Marlene Hofer
